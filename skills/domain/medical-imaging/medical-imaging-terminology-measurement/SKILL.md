# Medical Imaging Terminology And Measurement

## Workflow

1. Identify modality, anatomy, measurement target, acquisition context, and reporting standard.
2. Verify current terminology or measurement conventions when the output may guide clinical interpretation.
3. Separate image-processing measurements from diagnostic interpretation.
4. State units, plane, sequence/phase, segmentation method, uncertainty, and reproducibility limits.
5. Use structured-report language only within the verified standard's scope.
