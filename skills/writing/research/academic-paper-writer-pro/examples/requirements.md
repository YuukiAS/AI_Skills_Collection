# Formatting Requirements for Submission

## General Layout
*   **Paper Size**: A4 (210mm x 297mm)
*   **Margins**: Top/Bottom: 25mm, Left/Right: 20mm
*   **Columns**: Two-column format, column width 82mm, spacing 6mm.

## Fonts
*   **Main Text**: Times New Roman, 10pt.
*   **Title**: Arial, 24pt, Bold, Centered.
*   **Headings**:
    *   *Level 1*: Arial, 12pt, Bold, All Caps, Numbered (e.g., "1. INTRODUCTION").
    *   *Level 2*: Times New Roman, 10pt, Bold, Numbered (e.g., "1.1 Background").

## Citations & References
*   **Style**: IEEE Numeric Style (e.g., [1], [2]).
*   **Reference List**: Sorted by appearance order.
*   **Format**: 
    *   *Journal*: Author(s). "Title of paper," *Abbrev. Title of Journal*, vol. x, no. x, pp. xxx-xxx, Abbrev. Month, year.
    *   *Conference*: Author(s). "Title of paper," in *Abbrev. Title of Conf.*, City of Conf., Abbrev. Month, year, pp. xxx-xxx.

## Figures and Tables
*   **Captions**:
    *   Figures: Times New Roman, 8pt, centered below the figure. Format: "Fig. 1. Caption text."
    *   Tables: Times New Roman, 8pt, centered above the table. Format: "TABLE I. Caption text."
