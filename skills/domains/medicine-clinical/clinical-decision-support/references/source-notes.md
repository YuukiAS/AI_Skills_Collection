# Source Notes

Last reviewed: 2026-06-19.

Use current authoritative sources for medical claims. Prefer, in order:

1. Local institutional policy or user-provided protocol when the user says it governs the work.
2. Regulator labels and safety communications such as FDA, EMA, MHRA, or equivalent jurisdictional authorities.
3. Current specialty society guidelines and living guidelines.
4. Systematic reviews and major randomized trials.
5. Observational evidence, registries, expert consensus, or mechanistic rationale.

Record source title, publisher, publication/update date, accessed date, population, jurisdiction, and limits. If a source may be outdated or jurisdiction-specific, say so in the output.
