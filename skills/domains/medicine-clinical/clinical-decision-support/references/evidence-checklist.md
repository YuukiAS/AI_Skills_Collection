# Evidence Checklist

- Define PICO, setting, population exclusions, and outcome time horizon.
- Separate guideline recommendations, trial evidence, observational evidence, and expert opinion.
- State evidence certainty and recommendation strength in plain language.
- Check whether the evidence applies to the user's population, biomarkers, comorbidities, age range, and care setting.
- Flag off-label, investigational, contraindicated, or jurisdiction-specific uses.
- Include uncertainty, harms, alternatives, and need for clinician review.
