# Treatment Plan Checklist

- Intended use and clinician-review status are explicit.
- Patient details are de-identified and limited to necessary facts.
- Goals are measurable and tied to follow-up intervals.
- Interventions distinguish medication, non-medication, monitoring, education, escalation, and referrals.
- Current-source checks are cited or flagged as required.
- Missing contraindication/safety data are not invented.
- The plan is concise unless the user requested a detailed protocol.
