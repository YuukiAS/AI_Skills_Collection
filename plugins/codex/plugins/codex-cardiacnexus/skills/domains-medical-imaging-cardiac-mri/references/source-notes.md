## Minimum source-check expectations

For clinical, publication, guideline, or standards-sensitive cardiac MRI claims, check current authoritative sources such as SCMR, AHA/ACC, EACVI, imaging-vendor documentation, or task-specific challenge documentation.

When reporting such claims, state:

- source date or access date;
- whether the claim is workflow/QC guidance, research-method positioning, or clinical interpretation;
- the boundary of applicability, such as modality, sequence, population, scanner/protocol, or task type;
- whether clinician review is required.

This skill provides workflow and validation boundaries. It is not medical advice. Patient-specific interpretation, diagnosis, treatment implications, or clinical decision-making require clinician review and current authoritative sources.
